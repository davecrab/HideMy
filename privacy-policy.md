# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** December 2025

## Overview
This app is designed with privacy as a core principle. It does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data.

## Data Collection
The app does **not** collect any user data.

Specifically:
- No personal information is collected
- No analytics are used
- No crash reporting is enabled
- No identifiers are created or tracked

## Data Storage
All functionality is performed **entirely on-device**.

Any data created or used by the app:
- Remains local to your device
- Is never transmitted to external servers
- Is never shared with third parties

The app does not operate any servers or backend services.

## Network Usage
The app does not communicate with any remote servers and does not send or receive data over the network.

## Third-Party Services
The app does not use any third-party SDKs, services, or libraries that collect data.
